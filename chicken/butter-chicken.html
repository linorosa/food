Butter Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken
800g boneless chicken
Aromatics:
2 red onion, roughly cut
Marinate spices:
2 tbsp ginger paste 2 tbsp garlic paste 2 tbsp chili powder 2 tbsp salt
Sauce:
1 can coconut milk, just the fat 600g tomatoes, roughly slit 100g cashew 2 tbsp garlic paste 1 tbsp garam masala 4 tbsp swerve 4 tbsp chili powder 3 tbsp white vinegar

Instructions

Prepare the chicken
  1. Mix marinate spices ingredients on a large bowl. Cut chicken into thin slices.
  2. Add into marinate bowl and mix. Marinate for 15-20 minutes.
Cooking
  1. On a Dutch oven, fry marinated chicken until almost done, then reserve.
  2. Cook aromatics. Add in sauce and cook simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Remove and blend, then run it through a mesh strainer.
  4. Add back the sauce, chicken and 8 tbsp butter. Simmer for 5 to 7 minutes.
