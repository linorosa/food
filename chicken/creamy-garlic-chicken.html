    1. marinated chicken

    Sear chicken on a Dutch Oven on both sides, skin side down first. Then remove.

    1. butter

    Melt butter. Add onions and cook for about 5 minutes until it starts to caramelize.

    1. galic

    Add garlic and cook until it begins to get fragrant (about 1 minute).

    1. flour
      2 tbsp

    Add flour and cook for about 1 minute.

    1. chicken broth
      1 cup

    Add broth and cook until mixture thickens into a gravy.

    Add and mix well. Let it cook for about 1 minute.

    1. reserved chicken

    Add back the chicken. Baste it with the sauce and cook without lid in the oven at 400F for 45 minutes until chicken is at least 165F.
