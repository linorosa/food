Beet Carrot Salad

Ingredients

  • Beets
    1 lb
  • Carrots
    1 lb
  • Shallot
    1
  • Baby Arugula
    • Pantry basics:
  • White wine vinegar
  • Honey
  • Olive oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Prep

1
  • Beets
    1 lb
    Peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch thick wedges
  • Carrots
    1 lb
    Peeled and cut on bias into 1/4-inch thick slices
  • Oil
    2 tbsp
  • Salt
    1/2 tsp
  • Pepper
    1/4 tsp
  • Sugar
    1/4 tsp
Mix veggies thoroughly in a bowl
2
  • Shallot
    1
    minced
  • Olive oil
    1 tbsp
  • White wine vinegar
    2 tbsp
  • Honey
    1 tbsp
On a separate bowl, prepare vinaigrette

Cook

Warm the oven to 500F with the baking sheet inside
Roast veggies at 500F for 20-25 minutes
Mix with vinaigrette when serving. Serve cold with arugula
