Sesame Noodles

Ingredients

Main:
1/2 lb whole wheat spaguetti 250g plain baked tofu, cubed 1 scallion, sliced
Sauce:
3 tbsp soy sauce 3 tbsp rice vinegar 2 tbsp hoisin sauce 1 tbsp sesame oil 1.5 tsp honey

Instructions

  1. Cook spaguetti according to instructions, mix with the rest of the ingredients.
