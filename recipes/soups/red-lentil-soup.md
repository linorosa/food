
---
title: Red lentil soup
source: 
---

1) Add to pan

```
2 tbsp olive oil
3 onions, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
3 stalks celery, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 can coconut milk
1.5 cup diced tomatoes
1 quart chicken broth
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 tsp cumin
1 tbsp tumeric
1 cup red lentils
```

2) Simmer until carrots are soft.

3) Stir in:

```
4 cups spinach
1 tbsp lemon juice
```
