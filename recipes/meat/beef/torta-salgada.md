---
title: Torta Salgada
---

1. Prepare the dough, mixing thoroughly

```
3 cups  almond flour
1 cup   water
1/2 cup olive oil
6       eggs
4 tbsp  parmesan
2 tsp   salt
```

2. Add and mix

```
2 tbsp baking powder
```

3. Prepare the filling and mix in with the dough

```
3 lb cooked ground beef
frozen veggies
```

4. Bake 40-45 minutes at 350F on a parchement-lined square baking sheet.
