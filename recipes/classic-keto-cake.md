title: Classic Keto Cake
source: 
---

1. Preheat oven to 350F and grease an 10-12 inch bundt pan

2. Combine on a large bowl

```
1/2 cup butter
5       eggs
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup milk
2 tsp   baking powder
```

3. Add in

```
2 cups  almond flour
3/4 cup swerve
```

4. Bake 40 minutes. Wait 15 minutes before flipping.
