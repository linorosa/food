Ponzu Sauce

Ingredients

500ml rice vinegar 400ml soy sauce 100ml lemon/yuzu juice (optional) 50ml white wine (*) 1/4 tsp sugar 10g bonito flakes 10g dried kombu

(*) the white wine and sugar are a substitute for mirin. Could also use sherry instead

Instructions

  1. Mix everything on a sealed container and keep on the fridge for 2 days.
  2. Strain to remove kombu and bonito flakes.
