Tzatziki

Ingredients

  1. greek yogurt
    2 cups
  2. cucumber
    1 large
    peeled, grated, tossed with 1/2 tsp salt
  3. garlic
    4 cloves
    very finely minced
  4. lemon
    1/2
    just the juice
  5. dill and/or mint
    3 tbsp

Steps

After the salted cucumber sits for around 10 minutes, squeeze it dry on cheesecloth or a high quality paper towel. Mix all ingredients together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
