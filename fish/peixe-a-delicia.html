Peixe a Delicia

Ingredients

Fish:
1 kg fresh tilapia fillets 5 cloves garlic, mashed juice of 1 lime
Sauce base:
1 onion 2 tbsp tapioca flour 2 cups cooking cream
The rest:
4 bananas, cut lengthwise cheese, shredded

Instructions

  1. Season fish with lime juice and galic. Rest on a bowl for 15 minutes, then sear it on a large skillet and set aside.
  2. Sear sliced bananas. Then set aside.
  3. Sear onion until golden. Add flour and mix well. Add cooking cream and mix, heating to incorporate.
  4. Layer up ingredients on a large baking dish. First 2/3 of sauce at the bottom, then fish, banana, 1/3 of sauce, and finally cheese at the top
  5. Roast on the oven at 400F for 15 minutes.
