Fried Anchovies

Ingredients

Main:
60g frozen dried anchovies 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
Sauce:
1 garlic glove, minced 1 tsp soy sauce 1 tsp water 1 tbsp brown sugar
Finishing:
1 tsp toasted sesame oil 1 tsp toasted sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Sear garlic a bit. Add anchovies and mix. Then add the peanutes and sear some more until peanuts are crunchy and anchovies are light brown.
  2. Add the sauce and mix well, cooking for another minute.
  3. Add sesame oil and turn off the heat.
Based on Maangchi
