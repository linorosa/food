# Classic Keto Cake

_From [keto connect](https://www.ketoconnect.net/breakfast-bundt-cake)_

1. Preheat oven 350F and grease a 10-12 bundt pan.

2. Combine on a large bowl.

	- 1/2 cup **butter**
	- 5 **eggs**
	- 1/4 tsp **salt**
	- 1/2 cup **milk**
	- 2 tsp **baking powder**

3. Add in:

	- 2 cups **almond flour**
	- 3/4 cup **swerve**

4. Bake 40 minutes. Wait 15 minutes before flipping.
