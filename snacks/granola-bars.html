Granola Bars

  1. rolled oats
    2 cups (180g)
  2. almonds
    1 cup (140g)
    chopped
  3. date paste
    1/2 cup (150g)
  4. peanut butter
    1/4 cup (65g)
  5. dark chocolate
    2 oz (50g)
    melted

  1. To make the date paste, just soak some dates in water and blend them on a food processor until smooth.

    1. rolled oats
      2 cups (180g)
    2. almonds
      1 cup (140g)
      chopped

    Toast on a large skillet for about 10 minutes.

    1. rolled oats
      2 cups (180g)
      toasted
    2. almonds
      1 cup (140g)
      toasted
    3. date paste
      1/2 cup (150g)
    4. peanut butter
      1/4 cup (65g)
    5. dark chocolate
      2 oz (50g)
      melted

    Mix everything on a large bowl

  4. Place the mixture on a parchement-lined 8x8 inch baking dish and press on top to compact it a bit. Alternatively could lay them on a muffin tin.

  5. Leave on the fridge for at least 2 hours, but ideally overnight. Then remove and cute into bar shapes.
