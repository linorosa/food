Cook

Mix and blend on a food processor:
  1. hazelnuts
    1 cup (130g)
    roasted and blended into flour
  2. pitted dates
    1/4 cup (75g)
    blended into paste
  3. dark chocolate
    (50g)
    melted
  4. cocoa powder
    1 tbsp
  5. maple syrup
    1 tbsp
  6. whipping cream
    3 tbsp
Bring paste into a large bowl and fold in the egg and the oat flour:
  1. egg
    1
    room temperature
  2. oat flour
    1 cup (100g)

Place the dough on the fridge for 20 minutes.

Roll the dough into 10 balls and place them on a parchement-lined baking sheet. Press them down but not too thin.

Optionally form a well in the middle of the cookie at this point and add some Nutella paste or jam into it after done.

Bake for 8-10 minutes at 350F and remove to cool
