Almond Butter Cookies

Ingredients

    Pantry basics:
  1. swerve
    3/4 cups
  2. almond flour
    2 cups
  3. baking powder
    1 tsp
    4. Fridge basics:
  4. butter
    1/2 cups
    melted
  5. egg
    1
    beaten
  6. vanilla
    1 tsp
Dough

Combine on a large bowl:

  1. butter
    1/2 cups
    melted
  2. swerve
    3/4 cups
  3. egg
    1
    beaten
  4. vanilla
    1 tsp

Add flours slowly and mix thoroughly:

  1. almond flour
    2 cups
  2. baking powder
    1 tsp
  3. salt
    a pinch

Wrap up the batter and freeze for 1 hour

Baking

Form the batter into circles

Press the dough with a fork

Bake at 325F on a parchement-lined baking sheet for 10-15 minutes

Let them cool for at least 30 minutes
