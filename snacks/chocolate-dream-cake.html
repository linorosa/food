Makes 2 small ramekens worth

Cake batter

1
  • butter
    1 tbsp
    melted
  • egg
    1
  • vanilla extract
    1/4 tsp
Mix on a medium bowl
2
  • almond powder
    2 tbsp
  • baking powder
    1/2 tsp
  • cocoa powder
    1 tbsp
Add in and mix
3
Pour batter into 2 small greased ramekins
Microwave for 1 minute
Flatten the cakes
Poke several holes on the cakes

Chocolate ganache

4
  • sugar-free chocolate
    1/4 cup (55g)
  • milk
    3 tbsp
Mix on a small microwave-safe bowl
Microwave for 1 minute, then mix well
Pour into the cakes

Top Layer

5
  • sugar-free chocolate
    1/3 cup (40g)
  • butter
    1 tbsp
Mix on a small microwave-safe bowl
Microwave for 1 minute, then mix well
Pour into the cakes
Chill for 15 minutes
