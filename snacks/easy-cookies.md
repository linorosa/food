# Easy Cookies

_From [nom nom paleo](http://nomnompaleo.com/post/140290900313/the-worlds-easiest-cookies)_

1. On a large bowl, mix flours

	- 2 cups **almond flour**
	- 1/2 tsp **baking powder**
	- 1/8 tsp **salt**
	- 1/2 cup **swerve**
	- 1/3 cup **butter**
	- 2 tsp **vanilla extract**

2. Add a bit of water, just until the mix is sticky enough to stay together.

3. Make cookies using 1 tbsp each.

4. Cook 20 min at 350F.

5. Meanwhile, slice chocolate thinly on a bowl. Melt it 30 seconds on the microwave.

	- 3.5 ounce **chocolate bar**

6. When cookies are completely cooled, coat each cookie, placing them chocolate side down on parchement paper. 

7. Put on fridge and wait until cold to remove.
