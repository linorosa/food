Butter Pecan Ice Cream

    Pantry:
  1. bocha sweet
    1/2 cup
  2. pecans
    1 cup
  3. salt
    1 tsp
  4. vanilla extract
    1 tsp
    5. Dairy:
  5. heavy cream
    2 cups
  6. milk
    1 cup
  7. butter
    4 tbsp

Day 1

Place the ice cream bowl on the freezer.

Butter Pecan

Melt butter on a skillet. Add pecans. Cook on medium-low until golden (6-8 minutes).

  1. pecans
    1 cup
  2. butter
    4 tbsp

Discard butter and store pecans on the fridge.

Liquids

Mix to combine using a hand mixer:

  1. bocha sweet
    1/2 cup
  2. salt
    1 tsp
  3. vanilla extract
    1 tsp
  4. heavy cream
    2 cups
  5. milk
    1 cup

Store it on the fridge.

Day 2

Pour liquids on the ice cream maker bowl. Using a hand mixer, mix on the lowest setting for 20 minutes. Make sure to allow the borders to freeze slightly and scrape the frozen bits from time to time with either the mixer or a spoon. At the end it will be with a soft serve consistency.

Add in the butter pecans and mix a minute more.

Place it on the freezer. It should assume the final consistency overnight.
