Butter Cookies

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:
1 1/4 cup almond flour 1/4 cup coconut flour 1/2 cup swerve 1 tsp baking powder 6 tbsp butter splash vanilla pinch salt
Liquid Ingredients:
6 tbsp butter splash vanilla

Instructions

  1. Mix on a large bowl:
    1 1/4 cup almond flour 1/4 cup coconut flour 1/2 cup swerve 1 tsp baking powder pinch salt
  2. Add and mix with hands:
    6 tbsp butter splash vanilla
  3. Form 12 balls and distrubute on parchement-lined baking sheet. Press them with a fork to form cookies. Cook at 350F for 11-14 minutes.
  4. They'll be very soft and brittle when they come out. Let them cool for an hour and store on the fridge.
