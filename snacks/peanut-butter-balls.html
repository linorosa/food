Peanut Butter Balls

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
    1/2 cup (130g)
  • confectioners swerve
    1/3 cup (50g)
  • coconut flour
    1/4 cup (35g)
  • almond flour
    1/4 cup (35g)
  • semi-sweet chocolate chips
    1/3 cup (42g)
  • butter
    1 tbsp (14g)

Balls

1
  • peanut butter
    1/2 cup (130g)
  • confectioners swerve
    1/3 cup (50g)
  • coconut flour
    1/4 cup (35g)
  • almond flour
    1/4 cup (35g)
Mix well on a medium bowl, and form a large ball
Form small balls, about 14
Place them on a bowl and freeze for about 35 minutes

Glaze

2
  • butter
    1 tbsp (14g)
  • semi-sweet chocolate chips
    1/3 cup (42g)
Add to a small microwave-safe bowl
Melt on microwave for 20-30 seconds

Mix

3
Dip balls on chocolate mix and place on a parchement-lined baking sheet, chocolate side down
Refrigerate for about 1 hour
