Butter Pecan

Ingredients

Butter Pecan:
1 cup pecans 4 tbsp butter
Liquids:
1/2 cup bocha sweet 2 cups heavy cream 1 cup milk 1 tsp salt 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Day 1

  1. Place the ice cream bowl on the freezer.
  2. Melt 4 tbsp butter on a skillet. Add 1 cup pecans. Cook on medium-low until golden (6-8 minutes).
  3. Discard butter and store pecans on the fridge.
  4. Mix to combine using a hand mixer. Then store it on the fridge:
    1/2 cup bocha sweet 2 cups heavy cream 1 cup milk 1 tsp salt 1 tsp vanilla extract

Day 2

  1. Pour liquids on the ice cream maker bowl. Let it churn for 20 minutes.
  2. Add in the butter pecans and mix a minute more.
  3. Place it on the freezer. It should assume the final consistency overnight.

Chocolate

Ingredients

3/4 cup bocha sweet 2 cups heavy cream 2 cups milk 50g 100% chocolate bar 2 tbsp dutch-processed cocoa powder 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Day 1

  1. Place the ice cream bowl on the freezer.
  2. Place on a saucepan and warm slowly, whisking constantly, until lightly simmering.
    3/4 cup bocha sweet 2 cups milk 2 tbsp dutch-processed cocoa powder 1 tsp salt
  3. Turn off heat and add 50g 100% chocolate bar. Mix until incorporated.
  4. Add 2 cups heavy cream and mix well.
  5. Leave it on the fridge overnight.

Day 2

  1. Pour liquids on the ice cream maker bowl. Let it churn for 20 minutes.
  2. Place it on the freezer. It should assume the final consistency overnight.
