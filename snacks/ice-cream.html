    Mix on a large bowl

  2. Freeze flat on a ziploc bag.

  3. Once frozen, remove from freezer and crush it up in small pieces.

  4. Transfer into a food processor and blend it.

  5. It can be stored on the freezer in this form, on a container covered with plastic wrap touching the top of the ice cream.
