Microwave Keto Bread

Ingredients

18g almond flour 14gbutter, melted 2g baking powder 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients on a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Microwave for 90 seconds.
  3. This is a bit on the dry side, so I prefer to make a grilled cheese sandwitch out of it by slicing it in half.
