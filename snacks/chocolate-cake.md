# Chocolate Cake

1. Mix everything:

	- 1 cup **almond flour**
	- 1/2 cup **cacao powder**
	- 1/4 cup **coconut flour**
	- 1 tsp **baking soda**
	- 1/2 tsp **salt**
	- 3/4 cup **swerve**
	- 1/2 cup **butter**
	- 1/2 cup **heavy cream**
	- 4 **eggs** room temperature

2. Bake for 70 minutes at 350F.
