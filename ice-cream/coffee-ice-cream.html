Coffee Ice Cream

This recipe isn't fully fleshed out yet. It's a work in progress

Ingredients

440g heavy cream 460g milk 100g bocha sweet 1 to 2 shots espresso 1 tsp vanilla 40g dark chocolate, roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients and leave them on the fridge to cool.
  2. Churn in the ice cream maker for around 20 minutes.
