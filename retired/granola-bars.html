Granola Bars

Try this with some oats substitute: chia seeds, flaxseed, hemp hearts, or maybe just almond flour.

Ingredients:

140g almonds, rougly chopped 140g rolled oats 40g coconut flakes, unsweetened 150g date paste 65g peanut butter 50g dark chocolate, melted

Instructions:

  1. To make the date paste, just soak some dates in water and blend them on a food processor until smooth.
  2. Toast oats and almonds on a large skilled for about 10 minutes.
  3. Mix all ingredients on a large bowl. Place the mixture on a parchement-lined 8x8 inch baking dish and press on top to compact it a bit. Alternatively could lay them on a muffin tin. Leave on the fridge for at least 2 hours, but ideally overnight. Then remove and cute into bar shapes.
