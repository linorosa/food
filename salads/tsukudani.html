Tsukudani

Ingredients

Main:
10g dried kombu, hydrated and sliced thinly 20g dried bonito flakes, hydrated 1/2 daikon radish, sliced thinly 1 carrot, sliced thinly then blanched
Sauce:
2 tbspsugar 2 tbsprice vinegar 2 tbspsoy sauce 1 tbspsesame oil 2 tbspsesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix main ingredients with sauce. Serve cold
