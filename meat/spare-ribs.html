Beef Spare Ribs

Ingredients

    Meat:
  • beef spare ribs
    1 slab
    • Rub (spices):
  • salt
    4 tbsp
  • black pepper
    1 tbsp
  • white pepper
    1 tsp
  • cayenne pepper
    1 tsp
  • garlic powder
    1 tsp
    • Rub (liquids):
  • Dijon mustard
    2 tsp
  • white vinegar
    2 tbsp
This recipe needs marinating for 4 to 12 hours

Prepare the Rub

1
  • salt
    4 tbsp
  • black pepper
    1 tbsp
  • white pepper
    1 tsp
  • cayenne pepper
    1 tsp
  • garlic powder
    1 tsp
Mix on a bowl
2
  • Dijon mustard
    2 tsp
  • white vinegar
    2 tbsp
Mix on a separate bowl

Prepare the Meat

3
  • beef spare ribs
    1 slab
Place ribs on a foil-lined baking sheet. Turn rubs over so meat side is down. Make some small slashes every few inches on the membranes that cover the ribs.
Brush about 1/2 of the Dijon mustard and sprinkle about 40% of the rub. Turn over and brush the rest of the Dijon mixture and the rub.

Marinate

4
Refrigerate 4 to 12 hours before cooking.

Cook

5
Bake at 300F for 1:30 hours. Remove and baste with the pan drippings. Cook for another 1:30 hours until very tender.
Remove and baste again. Let rest for 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
