Chili

Ingredients

Aromatics
1 onion, thinly sliced 2 cloves garlic, chopped
Peppers
1 green bell pepper, thinly diced 1 yellow bell pepper, thinly diced 1 jalapeno, thinly diced
Beef
1 kg ground beef
Liquids
1 cup bone broth 1 can (240ml) tomato paste
Spices
2 tbsp chili powder 1 tsp cumin 1 tsp garlic powder 1 tsp oregano 2 tbsp salt 1 tsp pepper
For serving
sour cream, to taste

Cook

  1. Cook aromatics on a Dutch oven. Onions first, then garlic.
  2. Add peppers and cook until soft.
  3. Add beef and break it down. Cook until mostly cooked.
  4. Add liquids, then spices. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Bring to boil on medium high. Cook covered, slightly ajar, on low for 15-20 minutes.
