Beef Stew

Ingredients

  • chuck-eye beef
    3 lbs
  • portobello mushroom
    1 large
  • vegetables
    to taste
    could be potatoes, carrots, parsnips, etc
    • Aromatics:
  • yellow onions
    2
  • garlic
    3 cloves
    • Liquids:
  • dry red wine
    1.5 cups
  • chicken broth
    4 cups
    • Herbs:
  • dried thyme
  • bay leaves
    • Pantry basics:
  • all-purpose flour
  • tomato paste
  • beef stock cube
  • salt
  • pepper

Prep

1
    Mushroom bowl:
  • portobello mushroom
    1
    Cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
    • Aromatics bowl:
  • yellow onions
    2
    finely chopped
  • garlic
    3 cloves
    minced
  • dried thyme
    1 tsp
    • Veggie bowl:
  • vegetables
    to taste
    roughly chopped

Cook

1
  • chuck-eye beef
    3 lbs
    seasoned with plenty of S&P
Sear on both sides, then set aside.
2
  • mushroom bowl
Cook covered on a Dutch oven until softened and wet (about 5 minutes)
3
  • aromatics bowl
Add and cook until onions are soft and mushrooms are dry and browned
4
  • all-purpose flour
    3 tbsp
  • tomato paste
    1 tbsp
Add and cook until lightly browned (1 minute)
5
  • dry red wine
    1.5 cups
Slowly whisk in whine. Bring to a simmer
6
  • chicken broth
    4 cups
  • beef stock cube
    1
  • bay leaves
    2
Add and bring to a simmer
6
  • chuck-eye beef
  • salt
    2 tbsp
  • pepper
    2 tbsp
Add and bring to a simmer. Cook slightly ajar at 280F for 1.5 hours
7
  • veggie bowl
Add and cook for another 1.5 hours
