Dashi

Ingredients

10g dried kombu 20g dried bonito flakes 1L water

Instructions

  1. With a wet paper towel, wipe dust from kombu.
  2. Let kombu soak covered on the sauce pan with water for 1 hour.
  3. Heat on medium-low uncovered. Do not bring to a boil. When water reaches 176F, and some bubbles start forming at the bottom, remove kombu and reserve.
  4. Turn off heat and add bonito flakes. Do not touch or stir. Just let it rest there for 5 minutes.
  5. Drain on a strainer lined with paper towel. Don't press or squeeze.
    6. Don't throw away used kombu and bonito flakes.
    Reuse them to make Tsukudani.
