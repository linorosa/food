Potato Leek Soup

Ingredients

  • leeks
    4
    white and light green parts, chopped
  • garlic
    2 cloves
    peeled and smashed
  • yukon gold potatoes
    2 lbs
    peeled roughly chopped
  • chicken broth
    7 cups
  • fresh thyme
    3 sprigs
  • heavy cream
    1 cup
The quantities are mostly a guideline. I do it with sometimes more potatoes, sometimes less leeks and it's all good.
1
  • leeks
    4
    white and light green parts, chopped
  • garlic
    2 cloves
    peeled and smashed
On a Dutch oven, cook until soft and wilted - about 10 minutes
1
  • yukon gold potatoes
    2 lbs
    peeled roughly chopped
  • chicken broth
    7 cups
  • fresh thyme
    3 sprigs
Add and bring to a boil
Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, or until potatoes are soft
1
Fish out the thyme and puree the soup
1
  • heavy cream
    1 cup
Add and bring to a simmer
Add salt and pepper to taste
